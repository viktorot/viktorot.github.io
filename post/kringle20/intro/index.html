SANS Holiday Hack 2020

The holidays are time for friends, family and most of all, doing CTF challenges.

So when this year’s holidays rolled up (technically last year’s, because this will be published in 2021), I couldn’t miss the opportunity to participate in SANS Holiday Hack challenge, having missed it the prior year. The Holiday Hack challenge is one of the most fun CTF challenges that one can participate in around the holidays.

The whole challenge is structured around a mysterious villain, trying to ruin Christmas. It is our job to find out who the villain is and save Christmas.

The CTF consisted of 12 main objectives, 7 terminal challenges and 4 mini games. The challenges were accompanied by video talks as part of KringleCon, that introduced new topics and provided hints for some of the challenges.

In the next few posts I’m gonna write about the challenged them self and how I went about solving them.
